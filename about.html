About SmartCert

Need Help? Contact us @ contact@smartcert.ie. We’re SmartCert—a platform dedicated to helping 5th and 6th year Leaving Cert Biology students excel. Our chatbot offers quick Q&A, and our notebook feature lets you save useful responses. We aim to make studying simpler, more interactive, and fun! All of our advanved AI models are trained on leaving certificate specific context - ensuring you get exam focused answers when you need them. We are built by students - students who wanted to give back and help our younger friends. Take this opptunity to increase your study productivty, and become a H1 student today!
