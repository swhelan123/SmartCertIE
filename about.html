About SmartCert

SmartCert is the only AI-driven study platform dedicated to the Leaving Cert curriculum. By focusing on exam-focused content, we help you learn exactly what you need—no time wasted on irrelevant topics.

Key Features

  • LC-Focused AI: Our chatbot is trained on the official syllabus and exam patterns.
  • Personal Notebook: Save important answers and revisit them anytime.
  • Topic-by-Topic Prep: Zoom in on exactly the chapters you need.
  • School Access (Coming Soon): We'll soon offer licenses for entire schools.
  • More Subjects Ahead: Biology is just the start—stay tuned for expansions!

Whether you're aiming for a H1 or just want a better grasp of the curriculum, SmartCert offers a straightforward way to revise. We streamline your study process so you can focus on what matters most.
